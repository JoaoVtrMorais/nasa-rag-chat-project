# Apollo 13 Mission Evaluation Dataset - 8 questions
# Format: question | reference_answer

What were the call signs used for the Command Module and Lunar Module when both vehicles were manned?|When both vehicles are manned, the call sign will be Odyssey for the CSM and Aquarius for the LM.

Who were the three crew members of Apollo 13 and their roles?|Commander (CDR): James A. (Jim) Lovell, Jr.; Command Module Pilot (CMP): John L. Swigert, Jr.; Lunar Module Pilot (LMP): Fred W. Haise, Jr.

At what Ground Elapsed Time (GET) did the crew report S-II ignition?|At 000:02:48 GET, the CDR reported "S-II ignition."

What was the predicted S-IVB cutoff time communicated by Houston around 11 minutes GET?|Houston communicated a predicted S-IVB cutoff time of 12 plus 34 (GET 00:12:34).

Why did the O2 FLOW HIGH light come on around 2 hours and 12 minutes GET, and what did Houston say?|The O2 FLOW HIGH light came on at approximately 2 hours and 12 minutes GET and remained pegged high. Houston stated it was nominal with the WASTE TANK VENT open and that it was "no sweat."

At what approximate GET did LM jettison occur during the return?|LM jettison occurred at approximately 141:30 GET (crew called "LM jettison" at 141:29:56, confirmed by Houston at 141:30:05).

What was the prime recovery ship and landing area for Apollo 13?|The prime recovery ship was the USS Iwo Jima in the Mid-Pacific landing area.

What was the final GET of moonset and the Moon-check attitude provided in the entry pad?|GET of moonset and Moon-check attitude: 142:38:19, 178 degrees.
